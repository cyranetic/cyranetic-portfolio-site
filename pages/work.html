Momentary was a collaboration with Michael Abstract, an electronic music composer based in Tokyo, Japan. In creating the visuals for this modular synth piece, I wanted to evoke a sense of creation and primal love. The pink/purple hues, and the amorphous rounded geometries, reference the safety, protection, and nourishment of the womb. Conversely, metallic fractal structures represent humanity's drive toward technology and self-destruction. Both natural and technological sides of humanity exist, although it is a mysterious symbiosis that could prove fleeting in the history of our species.
