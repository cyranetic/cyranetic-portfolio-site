Artists Have Unique Video Hosting Needs

When LFS is recommended online, it often states that if you want to back up large files with Git, you should use LFS. It neglects to say that there are times when it doesn't make sense to have large files in your project at all, and it neglects to say how difficult this is (especially for those newer to using git) to remove LFS tracking. Basically, LFS seems like an option for websites including large data files or binary files on the BACKEND. Not for photos/videos on the FRONTEND which will load slowly/break/disrupt UX. - benefits of vimeo: - https://vimeo.com/upgrade
